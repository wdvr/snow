Snow Tracker helps skiers find resorts with the best snow conditions by aggregating weather data and snow quality estimates.

No login is required. The app fetches real-time weather data from public APIs.

To test:
1. Launch the app to see the resort list
2. Tap a resort to view detailed conditions
3. Use the map view to explore resorts geographically
4. Check favorites and recommendations
5. View settings for preferences

The app works with live weather data and requires no special credentials.
