Track real-time snow conditions at ski resorts worldwide. Powder Chaser helps you find the best skiing conditions with accurate snow quality ratings.

FEATURES:
• Real-time snow conditions for 28+ resorts across 8 regions
• Snow quality ratings based on fresh powder since last thaw
• Temperature and snowfall data at multiple elevations
• Beautiful map view with resort locations
• Offline support with cached data

REGIONS COVERED:
• North America West Coast (Whistler, Mammoth, Tahoe)
• North America Rockies (Vail, Park City, Jackson Hole)
• Alps (Chamonix, Zermatt, St. Anton, Verbier)
• Japan (Niseko, Hakuba)
• Oceania (Remarkables NZ, Thredbo AU)
• South America (Portillo)

Perfect for planning your next ski trip or checking conditions before heading to the mountain.
