# Snow Tracker Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 27, 2026

Snow Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Snow Tracker mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** If you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, we receive basic profile information (name, email address) as permitted by those services
- **Preferences:** Resort favorites, unit preferences (temperature, distance, snow depth), and notification settings
- **Feedback:** Content you provide through our feedback system

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Location Data:** If you grant permission, we collect your approximate location to show nearby ski resorts
- **Usage Data:** App interactions, features used, and crash logs to improve performance
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and network information

### Information from Third Parties
- **Weather Data:** We retrieve weather and snow conditions from public weather services and resort APIs
- **Authentication:** When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive limited profile information as allowed by those platforms

## How We Use Information

### Core App Functionality
- Display personalized ski resort information and weather conditions
- Show nearby resorts based on your location (if permitted)
- Save your favorite resorts and preferences
- Provide offline access to recently viewed data

### App Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- Debug technical issues and crashes
- Understand which resorts and features are most popular

### Communication
- Send push notifications about snow conditions (if enabled)
- Respond to your feedback and support requests
- Provide important app updates and announcements

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

### Service Providers
- **Weather APIs:** We share resort location data with weather services to retrieve conditions
- **Authentication:** Apple and Google handle sign-in securely on their platforms
- **Analytics:** Anonymous usage data may be shared with analytics providers to improve the app

### Legal Requirements
- To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with advance notice)

### Anonymous Data
We may share aggregated, anonymous usage statistics that cannot identify individual users.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

- **Encryption:** Data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- **Secure Storage:** Personal data is stored securely on your device and in our systems
- **Access Controls:** Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Updates:** We regularly update our security practices and software

## Your Choices and Rights

### Account Control
- **Sign-In:** Account creation is optional; the app works without signing in
- **Data Deletion:** You can delete your account and associated data at any time through the app settings

### Location Data
- **Permission:** Location access is optional and can be enabled/disabled in system settings
- **Accuracy:** We only request approximate location, not precise GPS coordinates

### Notifications
- **Control:** All notifications can be disabled in app settings or system settings
- **Types:** We only send notifications about snow conditions, never marketing messages

### Data Portability
- **Export:** Request a copy of your personal data by contacting us at privacy@snowtracker.app
- **Format:** Data will be provided in a common, machine-readable format

## Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after deactivation
- **Usage Data:** Anonymous usage statistics may be retained longer for app improvement
- **Cached Data:** Weather and resort data is cached locally and automatically expires

## Children's Privacy

Snow Tracker is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

## International Users

Snow Tracker is operated from Canada and complies with applicable privacy laws. If you are located outside Canada, please be aware that information we collect will be transferred to and processed in Canada.

### European Users
If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to rectify inaccurate data
- Right to erase your personal data
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@snowtracker.app.

## California Privacy Rights

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

### Categories of Information Collected
- Identifiers (email address, device ID)
- Location data (approximate location)
- Usage information (app interactions)

### Your CCPA Rights
- **Right to Know:** Request information about data collection and sharing
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt out of sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
- **Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

To make a request, contact us at privacy@snowtracker.app.

## Third-Party Services

Snow Tracker integrates with third-party services:

### Weather APIs
- **Open-Meteo:** Open-source weather API (https://open-meteo.com/)
- **National Weather Services:** Government weather data providers
- **Resort APIs:** Official resort weather stations

### Authentication
- **Sign in with Apple:** Apple's privacy-focused authentication service
- **Google Sign-In:** Google's authentication platform

### Analytics
- **First-Party Analytics:** We use our own analytics to understand app usage
- **No Third-Party Tracking:** We do not use advertising networks or tracking services

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will:
- Notify you of significant changes through the app
- Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
- Provide 30 days' notice for material changes

Your continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

## Definitions

- **Personal Information:** Information that identifies, relates to, or could reasonably be linked with you
- **Anonymous Data:** Information that cannot identify you individually
- **Third Party:** Any entity other than Snow Tracker and its users
